There is something new, The Party Center!

07/08/2025

Have you ever wanted to make a party but your house wasn't as big as you needed to make it? Or you want to have a theme party and you don't know how to decorate a place? Then definently this is wath you needed, The Party Center, in where you can do all this.

People always want to celebrate their birthdays or maybe just want to make a party; lately it's getting popular to make theme parties, especially in kids. That's why Sussan Something create this amazing place!
I'm mother of two little boys and they always wanted to have big parties in their birthdays, and not a normal birthday with just a cake, they wanted a big decorated party with their friends, but I didn't had the space to make them what they wanted, thar's I created this place. We were talking with Susan, and she told us that this wasn't easy, she had a really good job, but decided to leave to make her new dream come true. If we were able to count how many people make a party every single week-end definetly there'll be hundreds, that's why The Party Center it's getting popular.

Now, where is this amazing place!?

This amazing place is just in La Presidenta park, it's in a strategic place because it has access to main streets and it's about 15 minutes walking from the Metro Station, beside it's in the Poblado, one of the main areas of Medellin.

How can I make a party here?

There are many points to be considered:

1. Everyone can make here their party but but the party must not be bigger than a 100 people.
2. You have to sign up on the center's web page, or in their social media you can also find a link to sign up, you must follow all the instructions(to have the best experience possible.
3. To sign up you have to pay depending on how big is your party and all the services you use from the center, that money will be destined to the decoration and the organization of the party.
