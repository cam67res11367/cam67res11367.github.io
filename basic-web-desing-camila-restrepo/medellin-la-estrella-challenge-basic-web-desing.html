Medellín-La Estrella Challenge!

06/23/2025

Medellín is a city where you can find opportunities for everything. Next month, everyone has the opportunity to accept a new challenge, The Medellín-La Estrella race!

The mayor’s office always promotes using environmentally friendly vehicles and having healthy habits, the best way to promote both is doing a bike race.
This way we help the environment, have healthy habits and we can have fun with our friends and family, I usually go with my wife and sons! I invite you all, Metropolitan Area, to be part of the biggest bike race you will ever see! This is what the mayor said when the race was announced. Every year hundreds of people sign up to these races, but this year we’ll see the biggest race in Medellín history, so large that the race goes all through the Metropolitan Area.

But, where is going to go?

The race will start at the Botanical Garden, will pass through Botero Square, River Parks and then through avenue 63 to arrive at the south highway; to finish the race the participants have to get to the end of the highway, enter La Estrella and get to its main park.

How can I be part of the race?

There are many points to be considered:

1. Everyone can be part of the race! Maybe not everyone has to be on a bike, but watching the race can be part of the fun.
2. You have to sign up on the city’s web page, or in their social media you can also find a link to sign up, you must follow all the instructions.
3. Part of the instructions for the inscription include putting your real age (this affects where and when you start the race) and claiming your equipment(bottle of water, t-shirt, stickers, and more surprises).
4. To sign up you have to pay 100.000 pesos, that money will be destined to the equipment and the organization of the event.
5. The most important thing, have the emotions to be part of this race!

Now that you know more about this race, will you be part of it?
